FAQ
Common Questions
How is TROFi Ride different from Other Ride-Hailing Apps?+
TROFi offers zero surge pricing at all times — even during peak hours, rain, or festivals. We also offer a unified wallet plus TROFi Coins on every trip.
Are the drivers background-verified?+
Yes. Every TROFi driver undergoes police verification, identity check, and a mandatory safety training module before being onboarded.
What happens if I cancel a ride?+
You can cancel without charge within the first few minutes of booking. After that, a minimal cancellation fee applies.
Is intercity cab travel available in all cities?+
Intercity cabs are currently available from all active TROFi cities to regional destinations.
